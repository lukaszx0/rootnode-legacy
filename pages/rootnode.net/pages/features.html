Feature matrix

We are bulding our services from scratch. It takes a hell of a lot of time but we have big plans and we are very eager to get everything back.

Currently, our users have only access to Web containers. In the near future we are planning to run the Fastweb platform in order to take over heavy traffic of the web applications written in Ruby, Python and PHP.

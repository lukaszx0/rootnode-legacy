Contact

Talk to us with mailing list, IRC channel or e-mail

Contact us

There are many ways to contact us. However, you must follow specific rules to get a reply quickly. As we do not provide official support and e-mail can take some time to get answered, it is better to start from the mailing lists.

E-mail

You can contact as by e-mail.
admins at rootnode.net

Before sending e-mail make sure that we know

  • Who? Give us your uid and/or username.
  • Where? Give us servern ame or result of ip command.
  • What? Executed commands (with full input and output), real domain names, config files etc.

If you are already a Rootnode user, consider writing to mailing list first. It is very likely that you will get your answer sooner.

Mailing lists

We use mailing lists which is a really good place to ask for help. Be kind, describe your problem precisely and remember to attach at least following details:

  • uid and/or username
  • server name, e.g. web1
  • executed commands with full input and output
Important Do not hide any domain names because it will only make the problem harder to solve.

Subscribe

To subscribe to the list, send empty e-mail to listname+subscribe@lists.rootnode.net, e.g. users-pl+subscribe@lists.rootnode.net.

You will receive confirmation e-mail with further instructions.

Unsubscribe

To unsubscribe from the list, send empty e-mail to listname+unsubscribe@lists.rootnode.net, e.g. users-pl+unsubscribe@lists.rootnode.net.

You will receive confirmation e-mail with further instructions.

IRC channel

To talk to us, join #rootnode on irc.freenode.net network or connect now with IRC web client.

Couple of rules to make our lifes easier.

  • Be polite, be precise, be patient
  • Do not ask if there is any admin available. Other users probably know answer to your question.
  • Do not ask whether you can ask a question. Just ask.
  • Do not disconnect 1 minute after asking question. Nobody's that fast.

Notice It is very likely, you will not get any answer at all. Take into consideration that we can be in different time zone and we do not track IRC all day long.

About

All you want to know about Rootnode

Rootnode The best shell account with Ruby, Python and PHP

Rootnode is a very unusual shell hosting. Actually, we don't even like to call it hosting. We are social-oriented, we have subversive ideas and we are very eager to make this place unique.

We are social

We love to share knowledge and we like to work together. The most important part of Rootnode is its community. We destroy the scheme of lonely user in front of terminal. There are many other users you can communicate with in old-fashioned way using mailing lists and IRC channel.

We do open source

A lot of people think that open source means for free. It doesn't. We are not able to offer our services for free because of many expenses. Rootnode has to pay for colocation, server leasing, electricity and bandwidth.

As a social hosting, Rootnode decided to go open source by making everything we do, available for free in public git repositories which can be found at https://github.com/rootnode.

Currently, we use following git repositories:

  • lxc - linux containers related software, tools and configuration.
  • satan - shell account service manager.
  • scripts - small scripts and additional tools.
  • pages - this website and other Rootnode web pages.
Feel free to make this code better.

Our philosophy

Rootnode is a hobby project. We do it for fun. Never, ever consider Rootnode as a commercial and professional solution. We are untrustworthy, unrealiable and you better have a backup :)

No clients

Although, we take money from you, you are not a client and we will never treat you like one. Please, don't behave like one. You are a user and community member. Be nice to us and we will be nice to you.

No guarantee

We do not guarantee anything - no SLA, no uptime, no anything. You will not get your money back when something fails. You are warned! This is not a joke. If you are demanding by nature, please, do not purchase an account.

We do mistakes and servers fail but you can always check current status at http://rootnodestatus.net. We do not hide anything.

No official support

We are system administrators and we don't really have much time. Still, we read e-mails and we will probably answer yours but we cannot guarantee time when it happens.

However, there is a good news. We use mailing lists which is a really good place to ask for help. Be kind, describe your problem precisely and remember to attach at least following details:

  • uid and/or username
  • server name, e.g. web1
  • executed commands with full input and output
Important Do not hide any domain names because it will only make the problem harder to solve.

From scratch

We take all our experience and start again.

Rootnode has started in the year 2006. From that time, we have been giving our users a lot of freedom and access to a large number of services. Not only shell hosting but also application hosting, vpn, torrent, shoutcast and more.

Growing up in uncontrolled way has led us to multiple vulnerabilities and our systems were compromised eventually.

We decided to start over, rethink and do it from scratch. Needless to say, such process takes a lot of time and tremendous effort. For that reason, many services are currently unavailable, and must be redesigned with security in mind.

Rootnode was entirely colocated in datacenter in Amsterdam, Netherlands. We have invested a lot of money in own hardware. Unfortunately, we decided to power off this location and it is currently unavailable waiting for a huge maintenance and reopen. Right now, we are building new things on dedicated servers colocated in Europe.

Long story short.

  • We rerun our services one by one.
  • We use containers, a new virtualization technology to ensure user isolation.
  • We are working on fastweb platform to serve web traffic much faster.
  • We will kick ass again.
